## How RO works

Three steps. End to end.

**01 · Find** — Pulls fresh JDs from Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby. Scores each against your fit. Surfaces the matches.

**02 · Apply** — Drafts what each asks for: cover letter, spec, take-home, portfolio piece. RO drafts it, you review and send.

**03 · Track** — From first application to signed offer. RO tracks status, surfaces comp benchmarks, drafts negotiation points.

## FAQ

**Is the chat actually grounded, or is it making things up?**
RO only answers from the real JDs it has read. If something isn't in the data, RO will say so.

**Is it free?**
Early users get free access during beta. Pricing will be announced before your free period ends. No card to join the waitlist.

**How does RoleOS know which companies to track?**
For the public index, we curated 86 AI-native companies. For your own version, you give it a list — any company with a Greenhouse, Lever, or Ashby careers page works.

**Does RO apply for me?**
RO can submit on your behalf where you've approved it — for any company with a Greenhouse, Lever, or Ashby careers page. You set the rules: auto-apply above a fit threshold, require approval below it. You see every application before it sends.

**What about my data?**
The public index is public job postings — nothing personal. Your CV, application notes, and search history stay on your machine.

**When does v1 open?**
We're opening founding-user access in waves over the coming weeks.

**Who is this for?**
Senior PMs and PM-adjacent ICs — TPM, BizOps, Strategy — targeting AI-native companies. Narrow on purpose. Once we serve this group well, we'll expand.

**Will you spam me?**
No. One email when v1 opens. Maybe one more if there's something genuinely worth saying. Unsubscribe with one click.
