## What the role involves

**Surface:** Global equity programs including RSUs, PSUs, ESPP, capital markets transactions, M&A equity integration, Section 16 filings, and employee equity education

**Business model:** B2C / public company ride-sharing platform

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Legal, Finance, People / HR, Payroll, Total Rewards, Tax, Compensation Committee, Senior Leadership

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Accuracy and timeliness of equity award processing; SOX compliance control documentation; International equity program coverage and compliance; Employee education program reach and effectiveness
**Core responsibilities:**

- Support administration of Lyft's global equity programs including processing all awards, grants, exercises, cancellations and the issuance of all new grants in the US and in foreign jurisdictions
- Continue to drive system automation on the Schwab Equiview platform across Workday and ADP
- Develop and deliver world class employee education programs for new and continuing employees
- Support all aspects of SOX compliance and continuously work to build efficiencies around execution and documentation of key controls
- Engage with senior leadership from the legal, finance, and people organizations to design future equity programs and policies
- Coordinate closely with the payroll team to ensure proper withholding of tax from equity proceeds
- Define domestic and international integration strategies (deal specific) for equity, support M&A equity integration process
- Assist with D&O Questionnaires and Proxy disclosure regarding director and executive officer stock ownership
- Support the 10b5-1 program for executives and insiders across two brokers
- Support the timely filing of Section 16 reports (Forms 3, 4 and 5) with the SEC
