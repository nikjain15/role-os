## What the role involves

**Surface:** Flexdrive AV Operating Readiness program — fleet infrastructure, hiring, capital deployment, and market readiness for autonomous vehicle fleet ownership and operations

**Business model:** B2B / fleet operations; Flexdrive is Lyft's wholly-owned fleet management subsidiary transitioning to AV fleet ownership

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Operations, Fleet, Strategy, Infrastructure, FP&A, Treasury, Legal, People, Flexdrive CEO, Lyft AV leadership, Flexdrive board

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Deployment milestone achievement; Market readiness scorecard progress; Investment spend vs. capital deployment schedule; Cross-functional dependency resolution rate
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own and maintain the master program timeline for the Flexdrive AV Operating Readiness
- Develop and manage the program's RAID log (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies)
- Drive weekly leadership syncs, monthly deep-dive reviews, and quarterly board updates
- Translate upstream decisions into operational requirements and update the readiness roadmap in real time
- Serve as the connective tissue between workstream leads
- Build and deliver clear, concise status reporting to the Flexdrive CEO, Lyft AV leadership, and the Flexdrive board
- Develop market readiness scorecards
- Design and implement the program management operating model for AV readiness
- Build repeatable playbooks for market readiness that scale
