## What the role involves

**Surface:** Flexdrive fleet management operations (rental, vehicle remarketing, AV fleet buildout) across US and Europe; ~15,000 vehicles under management

**Business model:** B2B fleet management and rental operations, subsidiary of Lyft, expanding into AV fleet management

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** FP&A, Treasury, Accounting, Strategy, Infrastructure, Fleet Ops, external partners / vendors

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Forecast accuracy vs. actual spend; Cost-per-mile for AV operations; P&L impact of fleet management decisions; Margin improvement and cost reduction
**Core responsibilities:**

- Build and maintain complex financial models that forecast P&L impacts with accuracy
- Analyze performance trends across business lines for cost optimization and revenue enhancement
- Monitor industry conditions and market dynamics to surface risks and opportunities
- Own and optimize core business processes across fleet operations, vendor management, and financial operations
- Review actual spend against forecast and drive root-cause analysis
- Identify anomalies in complex invoice workflows and resolve discrepancies cross-functionally
- Build and maintain dashboards, reports, and tracking mechanisms
- Support month-end close deliverables including fleet/operational reporting, accruals, reconciliations, and variance analysis
- Develop cost-per-mile models, depot cost analyses, and unit economics frameworks for AV operations
- Serve as connective tissue between Business Operations, Finance, Fleet Operations, and external partners
