## What the role involves

**Surface:** End-to-end pricing lifecycle: base fare strategy, real-time dynamic pricing, rider engagement/incentives, and pricing platform infrastructure

**Business model:** marketplace / two-sided platform (rider demand, driver supply), B2C rideshare

**Team size:** team of PMs across four sub-teams (Planned Pricing, Dynamic Pricing & Offer Selection, Rider Engagement, Price Orchestration)

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Data Science, Analytics, Design, Finance, Marketing, Legal, Policy, Senior Leadership / Executives

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Gross Bookings; Active Riders; Adjusted EBITDA Margin; Rider affordability; Driver earnings; ROI of rider incentive spend
**Core responsibilities:**

- Manage and grow a team of product managers, providing development, mentorship, and coaching
- Drive strategic product and technology vision for Pricing, owning the system that directly impacts Gross Bookings, Active Riders, and Adjusted EBITDA Margin
- Set and align on business goals for pricing optimization with executives and business teams
- Incorporate end-to-end impact of pricing decisions on riders, drivers, and network growth
- Define short-term and long-term strategy, set team success metrics and roadmap
- Develop and execute product roadmap via collaboration with senior leadership and cross-functional partners
- Review how PMs translate customer needs into functional and technical requirements
- Guide data science, analysts, and engineers to refine scope, prioritize projects, and define opportunities
- Serve as an inspiring communicator in team all-hands and executive reviews
