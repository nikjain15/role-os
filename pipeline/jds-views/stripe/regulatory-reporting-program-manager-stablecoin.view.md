## What the role involves

**Surface:** U.S. regulatory reporting program for stablecoin and digital asset activities within Stripe's Global Regulatory Reporting team (NORAM focus)

**Business model:** financial infrastructure platform; regulated payments and financial services, including stablecoin issuance and custody

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Legal, Compliance, Accounting, Business/Product, Data Analytics, Engineering, Finance

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Timely and accurate submission of regulatory filings; Exam and audit readiness; Data quality and reconciliation rates; Regulatory reporting program coverage across all applicable U.S. requirements
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own end-to-end U.S. regulatory reporting program for digital assets including stablecoin related financial activities
- Interpret U.S. regulatory reporting requirements applicable to stablecoins, digital assets, payments, and custody
- Manage the full regulatory reporting lifecycle from data sourcing through submission
- Ensure regulatory reports accurately reflect stablecoin-specific activities and risks
- Design and maintain a robust regulatory reporting control framework
- Partner with Engineering, Data, Finance, Compliance and Legal to improve data lineage, transparency, and automation
- Own regulatory reporting change management for new or evolving stablecoin regulations
- Develop and maintain regulator-ready documentation
- Serve as primary point of contact for regulatory reporting during U.S. regulatory exams, audits, and inquiries
