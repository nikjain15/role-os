**Business model:** internal operations / HR function at a B2B financial infrastructure platform

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** People Solutions, People Data Analytics & Research (PDAR), People Partners, Employee Relations & HR Compliance, POI leaders, People team leaders, Senior stakeholders (directors, VPs)

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Time-to-decision on intake requests; Requestor experience quality; Queue depth and roadmap health; Resource allocation efficiency; Portfolio alignment with SOKRs and 3-Year Plan
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own and operate the POI intake process end-to-end via Jira-based system
- Serve as the primary intake partner for People team requestors
- Facilitate regular intake and portfolio cadences including backlog grooming and roadmap reviews
- Identify systemic patterns and surface insights to leadership
- Apply strategic judgment to prioritize the POI portfolio against SOKRs and 3-Year Plan
- Build and maintain portfolio visibility and capacity reporting dashboards
- Design, build, and continuously improve an AI agent for intake automation
- Continuously improve the intake experience via documentation and templates
- Partner with POI and People team leaders on resourcing and prioritization conversations
