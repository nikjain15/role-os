## What the role involves

**Surface:** Global Enterprise Fulfillment Platform — integrating Instaleap acquisition capabilities (fulfillment, picking, delivery orchestration, marketplace) into a unified international offering for retail partners across North America, Latin America, Europe, and the Middle East

**Business model:** B2B2C SaaS/marketplace — enterprise fulfillment platform licensed to retailers, underpinning Instacart's 4-sided marketplace

**Team size:** 12 TPMs on immediate team within a broader 25-person TPM organization

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Product, Data Science, Design, Business, VP/C-suite leadership, Instaleap Bogotá-based teams, Commercial teams

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Zero disruption to ~100 existing international retail partner operations during integration; Cross-sell conversion from combined client base; Platform unification milestones across discovery, gap analysis, and evolution phases; Alignment and velocity across distributed NA and Bogotá engineering teams
**Core responsibilities:**

- Lead the strategy, definition, and high-quality execution of company-wide programs with high ambiguity, complexity, risk, and executive visibility
- Transform poorly understood problems into well-structured, cross-functional programs; architect scalable execution plans and operating mechanisms
- Drive deep technical alignment across Engineering and Product at org leadership level
- Generate and maintain cross-program roadmaps; proactively identify dependencies, risks, and tradeoffs
- Partner closely with VPs and executive leadership to align program goals to business strategy
- Establish and evolve program frameworks, processes, and evaluation criteria as reusable standards
- Mentor and onboard TPMs at varying levels
- Lead integration of enterprise fulfillment, picking, delivery orchestration, and marketplace capabilities into a cohesive global platform
- Serve as connective tissue between Instacart's NA engineering org and Instaleap's Bogotá-based teams
- Ensure continuity for nearly 100 existing international retail partners during integration
