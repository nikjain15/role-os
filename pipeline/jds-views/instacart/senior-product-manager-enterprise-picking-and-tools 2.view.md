## What the role involves

**Surface:** Enterprise Picking & Tools — in-store picking workflows, shopper-facing order management tools, and retailer configuration/monitoring tooling across pick & deliver, pickup, and delivery-only fulfillment models

**Business model:** B2B enterprise SaaS / marketplace platform; retail partner enablement

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Design, Data Science, Enterprise Sales, Retailer Success, Operations

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Picking accuracy; Fulfillment speed; Shopper satisfaction; Retailer NPS and retention; Feature adoption by retail partners
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own enterprise picking experiences — from how shoppers receive and action orders in-store, to the tools retailers use to configure, monitor, and optimize their fulfillment operations
- Build deep understanding of shopper workflows and retailer operational pain points to design tools that improve picking accuracy, speed, and shopper satisfaction
- Work closely with retail partners to ensure our tooling integrates naturally into their in-store environments
- Partner with enterprise sales and retailer success teams to support partner onboarding, drive adoption of new features, and contribute to retailer NPS and retention
- Define platform solutions that scale across retailer sizes, store formats, and fulfillment models — including pick & deliver, pickup, and delivery-only
- Represent the picking and tools roadmap with internal stakeholders and, where relevant, with external retail partners
