## What the role involves

**Surface:** Partner activation and early retention for partner-referred customers; end-to-end partner activation funnel, CS segmentation, and scaled onboarding programs

**Business model:** B2B SaaS, channel/partner-led acquisition motion

**Team size:** 6–8 Scaled Customer Success Managers

**Cross-functional partners:** CS leadership, Channel Partner Managers, Product, Growth, Sales, Partnerships, Data

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Activation rate for partner-referred customers; Early retention rate; CSAT scores; Multi-product attach rates; Team efficiency metrics; CSM coverage of partner-held accounts
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own activation and early-retention strategy for partner-referred customers
- Design CS segmentation strategies using data modeling and AI-driven scoring
- Align partner activation roadmap with company-level goals cross-functionally
- Identify whitespace opportunities to increase multi-product attach rates
- Build, iterate, and optimize scalable CS programs across thousands of customers
- Leverage AI tooling, workflow automation, and predictive analytics to diagnose activation bottlenecks
- Partner with Product to improve self-onboarding features and activation tooling
- Manage, coach, and develop a team of 6–8 Scaled CSMs
- Drive team performance against activation rate, early retention, and CSAT targets
- Build AI fluency across the team
- Hire, train, and enable new team members
