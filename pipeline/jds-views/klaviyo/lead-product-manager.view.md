## What the role involves

**Surface:** Orchestration experience — Flows, campaign editor, and workflow builder surfaces on the Klaviyo platform

**Business model:** B2B SaaS — ecommerce marketing automation platform

**Team size:** 8–10 engineers plus 1 co-PM in broader core product area

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Design, Data, Go-to-market, Support, Sales, Marketing

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Adoption of Flows and orchestration features; Engagement with workflow/editor surfaces; Gross churn reduction; Retention and lifecycle marketing program effectiveness for customers
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own product strategy and roadmap for key orchestration experiences across flows, campaigns, and the Klaviyo editor
- Lead the evolution of Flows, one of Klaviyo's most widely used automation capabilities
- Simplify complex marketing workflows into intuitive, scalable product experiences
- Partner closely with design to deliver thoughtful, high-quality UX for core product surfaces
- Work with engineering to build reliable, scalable systems that support sophisticated automation use cases
- Meet directly with customers (virtually and in-person) to gather feedback and deeply understand their workflow challenges
- Define and track success metrics including adoption, engagement, and gross churn
- Collaborate cross-functionally with data, support, sales, and marketing teams to ensure successful product adoption
