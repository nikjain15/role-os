## What the role involves

**Surface:** Klaviyo segmentation platform — lists, segments, audience definition, evaluation, and insights systems

**Business model:** B2B SaaS, marketing automation / CDP for ecommerce

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Data, Go-to-market

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Segmentation latency (P95); Segment evaluation success rate; Freshness and correctness SLAs; System reliability; Customer ROI / retention improvements driven by segmentation
**Core responsibilities:**

- Define and own the product vision and roadmap for Klaviyo's segmentation platform.
- Build and operate segmentation as a first-class data product, optimized for correctness, performance, and scale.
- Partner with engineering to improve system performance, including segmentation latency, reliability, and SLA adherence.
- Evolve the expressiveness of segmentation (e.g., nested logic, advanced conditions) to meet the needs of mid-market and enterprise customers.
- Ensure segment definitions are consistent, explainable, and resilient as data complexity grows.
- Develop capabilities that help customers analyze, iterate on, and optimize high-value audiences.
- Introduce recommendations and insights to guide customers toward effective segmentation strategies based on best practices and their own data.
- Define and track key metrics, including latency (P95), success rate, freshness and correctness SLAs, and system reliability.
- Lead cross-functional initiatives across product, engineering, data, and go-to-market teams to improve how audiences are defined and activated.
- Communicate product strategy, tradeoffs, and technical decisions clearly across stakeholders.
