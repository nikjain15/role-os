## What the role involves

**Surface:** Inbound demo requests and free Klaviyo accounts; smallest accounts from first touch through purchase

**Business model:** SaaS / PLG + sales-assisted; ecommerce marketing platform

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Sales, Business Development, Product, Customer Success, Marketing

**Primary metrics (inferred):** New logo acquisition (logo count); Free-to-paid conversion; ADS (Average Deal Size implied); One-call close rate; Pipeline velocity
**Core responsibilities:**

- Field inbound demand (demos, trials, free accounts, and product inquiries) via phone, email, and other channels
- Run consultative, decision-driven demos focused on one-call closes whenever possible for smaller opportunities
- Do targeted business discovery to understand goals, urgency, and fit
- Qualify in or out efficiently, so you know where to double-down and where to quickly move on
- Own and work a high-velocity pipeline, using strong prioritization and time management to maximize new logo acquisition
- Drive new logo acquisition as a primary objective
- Contribute to broader Growth team goals across free-to-paid conversion, logo count, ADS, and other core growth metrics
- Consult with customers to advise and design solutions based on ecommerce best practices and optimal utilization of Klaviyo's platform
- Conduct consultative qualification of prospects, and provide demonstrations of the platform
- Collaborate cross-functionally to improve overall customer experience and create a structured feedback loop
