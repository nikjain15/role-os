**Surface:** FinTech & Ops — payments infrastructure, risk/identity/underwriting, customer support, returns/resale, shared technology and experimentation infrastructure

**Business model:** B2B wholesale marketplace (retailers + brands), mixed platform and financial services

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Finance, Risk & Legal, CX, Operations & Fulfillment, Engineering & Data Science, Design / UX, Product Marketing

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Contribution Profit (high single digit % uplift); GMV / LTV; Cost-to-serve / contact rate reduction; Payment fee reduction / margin improvement
**Core responsibilities:**

- Define a 6–12 month roadmap balancing efficiency experiments across the portfolio with growth investments and platform investments
- Lead rigorous A/B, cohort and holdout experiments with pre-specified success criteria
- Co-own returns/markdowns, underwriting and IDV policy design, payment routing rules, limit adjustments
- Execute multi-provider routing and adoption of lower-cost payment rails
- Reduce contact rate without harming customer satisfaction by expanding virtual assistant capabilities
- Redesign and implement improved returns policies and partner on liquidation/resale flows
- Use a documented rubric to decide when to automate vs. invest in human-driven growth
- Empower operational teams to iterate on launches without consistent product development support
- Represent product development organization in key operational forums
