## What the role involves

**Surface:** Member Account Safety — authentication, MFA, account recovery, fraud investigation experiences, account takeover prevention, anti-scam/phishing/social engineering defenses

**Business model:** B2C financial services / national bank (mobile-first)

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Engineering, Design, Fraud Product team, Fraud Risk Center of Excellence, Multiple Product teams

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Reduction in account takeover (ATO) incidents; Reduction in fraud-related member complaints; Authentication success rate for legitimate users; Scam/phishing incident prevention rate
**Core responsibilities:**

- Articulate a comprehensive roadmap for the Member Safety domain, aligning with SoFi's broader objectives and executive-level reporting.
- Own authentication, MFA, and account recovery, ensuring that the first point of contact with SoFi is both seamless and secure.
- Build active awareness into risk checkpoints, defending against scams and social engineering attacks to high-risk surface areas such as crypto.
- Guide a vision to improve the member experience during active fraud investigations, reducing fraud-related complaints and maintaining trust during high-stress events.
- Design and implement robust defenses for sensitive profile changes, utilizing step-up challenges.
- Partner with Engineering to implement security layers that accurately identify suspicious behavior or potential account takeovers (ATO) without adding friction for legitimate users.
- Champion transparency and security education through products that drive safety awareness.
- Drive cross-functional collaboration with the Fraud Risk Center of Excellence, multiple Product teams, Design, and Engineering.
- Partner closely with the Fraud Product team to align on a unified defense strategy.
