## What the role involves

**Surface:** SoFi's real-time payments platform — FedNow, Fedwire, instant debit (Visa Direct, Mastercard Send), RTP, and future real-time rails; shared infrastructure serving Consumer, Commercial Banking, Invest, Lending, and other business lines

**Business model:** internal platform (B2B2C); 'AWS of Banking' model serving internal SoFi business lines and commercial clients

**Team size:** unclear

**Cross-functional partners:** Risk and Compliance, Operations, Engineering, Executive Leadership, Galileo (vendor), Federal Reserve (FedNow, Fedwire), The Clearing House (RTP), Card network partners (Visa, Mastercard), Consumer Banking, Commercial Banking, Invest, Lending

**Primary metrics (inferred):** Transaction success rate; Payment speed / funds availability; Cost per transaction; Fraud rates per rail; Failure rate reduction; Complaints per rail; Platform adoption across business lines
**Core responsibilities:**

- Own the vision, strategy, and roadmap for SoFi's real-time payments platform, including FedNow, Fedwire, instant debit (Visa Direct, Mastercard Send), and future real-time rails
- Scale real-time capabilities to new business lines and onboard new use cases
- Partner with Risk and Compliance and Operations to ensure real-time payments are secure, compliant, and resilient
- Manage vendor and partner relationships including Galileo, the Federal Reserve, The Clearing House, and card network partners
- Drive operational excellence — define SLAs, monitor platform health, reduce failure rates, and build operational tooling
- Define and instrument metrics; build data infrastructure to track platform health
