Why this exists

Finding the right role is a research problem disguised as a sales problem. Every JD is a 1,200-word requirements doc. Every company is a strategy puzzle. AI PMs deserve tooling that matches the rigor of the work itself — not another spreadsheet.

What RoleOS does

01 · Scope

Find every role that matters

Crawls ATS portals (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby) for your target companies. Filters to the roles you'd actually take.

02 · Extract

Read every JD like a PM

Claude reads each posting and extracts archetype, seniority, must-haves with verbatim quotes, salary, location, visa — into a strict schema. Zero hallucination by contract.

03 · Track

Run the funnel, end-to-end

From "scoped" to "offer." See where you stand, what's stalled, and where your time goes. The CRM your job search deserved.
