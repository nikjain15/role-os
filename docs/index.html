Questions

The things people ask before signing up.

Is it free?

For the first 50 founding users — yes, free for 6 months. After that we'll announce pricing before your trial ends. No card required to join the waitlist.

How does RoleOS know which companies to track?

You give it a list. RoleOS supports any company with a Greenhouse, Lever, or Ashby careers page — that covers most of the AI-native tech market. If your target uses a different ATS, tell us and we'll add it.

Does it auto-apply for me?

No. RoleOS does the research and the tracking. You still write the application, talk to the recruiter, own the conversation. Automating outreach burns bridges — we're not building that.

What about my data?

The current version runs locally on your machine — your CV, your application notes, your search history never leave your laptop. The web app is being built waitlist-first. The numbers you see in the case study are Nikhil's data, on his machine.

When does v1 open?

We're opening founding-user access in waves over the coming weeks. Drop your email and we'll email you when your wave is up.

Who is this for?

Senior PMs and PM-adjacent ICs (TPM, BizOps, Strategy) targeting AI-native companies. We're staying narrow on purpose — once we nail it for this audience, we'll expand.

Is this a real company or a side project?

Right now — a soft launch. Nikhil is the founding user, dogfooding the whole pipeline as he runs his own search. If demand shows up, we take it further. Honest about that.

Will you spam me?

No. One email when v1 opens. Maybe one more if there's something genuinely worth telling you. Unsubscribe with one click whenever.
